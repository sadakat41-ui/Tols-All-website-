About This Text to Speech Tool

This tool uses your browser's built-in speech synthesis engine to convert text into spoken audio. The available voices depend on your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari) and your operating system (Windows, macOS, Android, etc.).

Simply type your text, choose a voice from the list, and click "Speak".
